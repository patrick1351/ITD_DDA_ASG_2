GRASS

Grass is a common material that can be found naturally in the forest. You can use this material to craft all sorts of helpful tools such as a rope.

STICKS

Sticks are also materials that can be found naturally in the forest. This item can be useful for crafting objects such as an axe or a campfire.

STONE

Stones can also be found naturally in the forest. They can be used to craft an axe and also start a fire for the campfire.

ROPE

The rope can be made by putting 2 grass together. Once made, the rope is a material used to craft an axe

AXE

The axe can be crafted by putting the Rope, Stick and Stone together. It is used to chop down trees to get logs and leaves for building the tent.

CAMPFIRE

The campfire is core survival building when surviving. It will help create a lightsource during the night so you will be able to see at night. It can be crafted using 8 stones 5 sticks

TREE

Trees can be seen all over the forest. The tree can be chop down with an axe to produce Logs and Leaves for the Tent

LOG

Logs can be obtained by chopping down trees. Use this material to craft a tent.

LEAF

Leaves can be obtained by chopping down trees. Use this material to craft a tent.

TENT

A tent can be crafted using 2 logs and 7 leaves. It creates shelter, which is crucial for wilderness survival.
